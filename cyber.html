THIS PAGE IS DECICATED TO EXPLORING THE MEANING AND IDEA OF CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting digital systems, networks, programs, and data from cyberattacks, aiming to safeguard confidentiality, integrity, and availability against threats like malware, ransomware, phishing, and data theft, crucial for individuals and organizations to prevent financial loss, service disruption, and reputational damage in our connected world. It involves a blend of technologies, processes, and training for people, covering areas like network security, cloud security, IoT security, and disaster recovery, with growing demand for skilled professionals
